Keyboard

A musical keyboard is the set of adjacent depressible levers or keys on a musical instrument.

Guitar

The guitar is a fretted musical instrument that has six strings. It is typically played with both hands by strumming strings.

Carnatic

Carnatic music owes its name to Sanskrit term Karnātaka Sangītam denotes “traditional” or “codified” music.

Piano
